Aumatic is a company dedicated to the design and construction of machines and assembly lines. The company provides individual assembly stations as well as complete technological lines – designed according to customer requirements – based on own projects or commissioned documentation.

In order to meet these requirements, commissioned orders are executed by our own engineering staff as well as experienced and skilled workers at every stage of production. We have a well equipped workshop and surface necessary for building large technological lines.

Our offer is addressed mainly to manufacturers of automotive parts and components, manufacturers of home appliances and the electro-mechanical industry – at the same time we are open to cooperation with companies from other industries. As standard, we use PLC/PAC controllers of leading companies and HMI panels to communicate with the operator.

We also manufacture robotized stations, measuring, testing, and vision inspection machines based on standard PLC or PC in the NI LabVIEW, NI LabWindows ™ / CVI or NI Measurement Studio environment.

In addition, we offer our proprietary Traceability systems, which cooperate with machines and technological lines.
